Mohamed Abdelfattah

I am a computer vision Ph.D. student at EPFL VITA lab, advised by Alexandre Alahi. In my Ph.D. journey, I enjoy tweaking transformer-based models for self-supervised action recognition. Before that, I was a deep learning visiting research student at Boston University where I worked with Sarah Adel Bargal. I also worked with Mohamed Elhoseiny at the KAUST Visual Computing Center (VCC) as computer vision research intern. I received my B.Sc. in Computer Engineer from The American University in Cairo (highest honors, Dean's list) in 2022.
